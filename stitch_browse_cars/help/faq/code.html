Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for a car?

You can search for cars using the search bar on the home screen. Filter by make, model, year, and other criteria to narrow down your results.

How do I contact a seller?

Once you find a car you're interested in, you can contact the seller directly through the "Contact Seller" button on the listing page.

What payment methods are accepted?

Payment methods are determined by the individual seller. Please check the listing details or contact the seller for more information.

How do I schedule a test drive?

You can request a test drive by contacting the seller through the app. They will coordinate a time and place with you.

What is the return policy?

Return policies vary by seller. It's important to discuss and agree upon the return policy with the seller before making a purchase.
